Civil and Commercial Litigation

The combined experience, skill and talent of our civil and commercial litigation team has enabled us to offer advocacy in the fields of financial and commercial matters including contract law and the interpretation of commercial contracts, corporate insolvency, telecommunication matters, administrative law including judicial review proceedings, intellectual property and trade mark litigation, contentious land and probate matters, employment law, revenue law matters, local and international arbitration of commercial disputes and construction contracts, and among other areas major debt collection as well as personal injury claims. In addition, the organisation has a solid reputation of successfully handling complex family law matters.
