Rachel E. Kitson

Associate

Rachel Kitson was called to the Jamaican Bar in December 2019 and is an associate in the firm's Litigation, Real Property and Conveyancing departments. Her practice areas include: Civil and Commercial Litigation, Probate and Administration of Estates, Real Estate, and Conveyancing.

Prior to joining the firm, Rachel obtained her Bachelor of Laws Degree with honours from the University of the West Indies, Mona in 2017 and thereafter received a Certificate of Legal Education from the Norman Manley Law School.

She is a member of the Jamaica Bar Association and enjoys travelling in her spare time.
